// The Problem

Your vibe-coded product may work. That does not mean it is ready for a real buyer, partner, or security questionnaire.

Founders are shipping faster than ever with AI coding tools. That part is good. The dangerous part is assuming working code is the same thing as review-ready code.

Enterprise customers, investors, and technical diligence teams look for the boring stuff that AI often gets wrong: exposed secrets, weak auth flows, unsafe dependencies, missing guardrails, brittle architecture, and compliance gaps no founder meant to ship.
